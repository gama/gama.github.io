About Me

I'm a software engineer with over 15 years of hands on experience and a lifetime of passion for technology.

I've had the opportunity to work in the most diverse work environments -- from research labs, to maintaining open source projects used by several remotely distributed developers, to designing and operating enterprise-grade software for several companies.

Being a part of such a broad range of projects and environments allowed me to learn and directly work with several Computer Science areas -- just to name a few: SOA & microservices, data mining & analytics, computational finance, machine learning, computer vision and data visualization.

In terms of education, I hold a MSc and a BSc degree from UFMG. I have also complemented my academic/scientific formation working as a Research Assistant at Rutgers University, where I was also able to sharpen my English skills.

Outside of work, you're likely to find me reading a good novel, working out at the gym or playing soccer with friends.

If you'd like to know more about me, feel free to reach me at gustavo dot gama at gmail dot com.
